1. Train 18 / Vande Bharat Express (22435 / 22436 and 22439 / 22440)

The Vande Bharat Express, also known as Train 18 is an Indian semi-high-speed train. It runs between New Delhi and Varanasi and New Delhi and Sri Mata Vaishno Devi Katra. It is fully air-conditioned. Vande Bharat Express has many facilities such as automatic doors, rotatable seats, Wifi, CCTV cameras, etc. Vande Bharat Express was finally inaugurated on 15 February 2019. It is the fastest engineless train in India.
